About

I'm Stefen, a 15-year-old YouTuber passionate about using AI to recreate the musical genius of late rappers Juice WRLD and XXXTentacion. Join me on this creative journey as I blend technology and music to keep their legacies alive through AI-generated songs.

I started exploring AI-generated music out of sheer curiosity and admiration for the late artists. Over time, it has evolved into a passion project where I strive to honor their memory by producing high-quality content that resonates with their fans.

Beyond music, I enjoy diving into different aspects of technology, from coding to content creation. I believe in pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation, and I'm always on the lookout for new ways to express my ideas and connect with people around the world.
